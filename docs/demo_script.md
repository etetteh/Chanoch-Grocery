# Demo Video Script: "Grocery Scout in Action"

## Scene 1: The Problem
*   **Visual**: User is in a grocery store, looking frustrated at a shelf of expensive cereal.
*   **Audio (User)**: "Ugh, prices are so high, and I need something that actually fits my keto diet."

## Scene 2: The Solution
*   **Visual**: User taps the "Grocery Scout" button on their phone.
*   **Audio (Grocery Scout)**: "I see you're looking at that cereal. I know it's frustrating when prices are high, especially when you're trying to stick to your health goals. I've found a much better deal on a keto-friendly alternative at the store just two blocks away. Would you like me to add it to your list?"

## Scene 3: The Interaction
*   **Audio (User)**: "Actually, yes, please do that. And can you help me plan my meals for the rest of the week?"
*   **Audio (Grocery Scout)**: "Absolutely! I've added that to your list. Let me look at your health profile and the groceries you already have. I can generate a 3-day meal plan that fits your budget and diet. Should I go ahead and create that for you?"

## Scene 4: The Result
*   **Visual**: The app UI updates seamlessly to show the added item and the generated meal plan.
*   **Audio (Grocery Scout)**: "All set! Your meal plan is ready. You're going to save some loonies this week!"
